Hot Banana Water

Description

This was my grandmother's recipe. She made us drink it. Many fond memories. Serve warm in mugs with whipped cream and marshmallows for an extra treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart water
  • 2 banana peels

Steps

  1. Cut off the ends of the banana peels.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
  3. Add 1 banana peel to boiling water.
  4. Turn off heat, cover, and wait for 10 minutes.
  5. Add 1 more banana peel and wait 10 more minutes.
  6. Stir to incorporate flavors.
  7. Strain into mugs and serve warm.
